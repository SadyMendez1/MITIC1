How To Make Patty

Ingredients

  • Fluor
  • Meat
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables

Steps To Make Patty

  1. Knead the flour.
  2. Cook meat and vegetables and make them a sauce.
  3. Pass the roll over the dough and make a circle
  4. Add the sauce to the dough and make the patty with the mold
  5. Fry in he oil
